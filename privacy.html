KnowPaw is a pet health and activity journal designed to help pet parents record everyday care, health notes, recurring care, and shared pet care routines.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have when using KnowPaw.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address
  • Display name
  • Profile photo or avatar
  • Sign-in provider information, such as Apple Sign In, Google Sign-In, or email/password login

You may also use KnowPaw in Guest Mode. Guest Mode does not require an account.

Pet Information

  • Pet name
  • Pet type, breed, gender, birthday, or approximate age
  • Weight
  • Avatar or pet photo
  • Medical notes or care notes
  • Daily goals and recurring care schedules

Pet Care Records

  • Food and water
  • Pee and poop
  • Walks and play
  • Medication and supplements
  • Symptoms and medical notes
  • Grooming and care changes
  • Weight records
  • Photos attached to records
  • Health documents or files you choose to upload

Shared Care Information

  • Pet owner and caregiver relationships
  • Invite codes
  • Shared pet profiles
  • Shared care records and recurring care items

AI Insights

  • If you use AI Insights, KnowPaw may send relevant pet record summaries to Firebase AI Logic / Google AI services to generate a pet care summary.
  • AI Insights are intended to help summarize records. They are not veterinary advice, diagnosis, or treatment recommendations.

Device and App Information

  • App version
  • Device type
  • Crash or performance-related information
  • Sync status and error information

2. How We Use Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Provide the core pet journal features
  • Save, display, and organize pet records
  • Sync your data across devices when you are signed in
  • Restore your data after signing back in
  • Enable shared care with trusted caregivers
  • Send local reminders and recurring care notifications
  • Generate optional AI summaries
  • Improve app reliability, performance, and user experience
  • Provide customer support

3. Cloud Storage and Sync

If you sign in, KnowPaw uses Firebase services to store and sync your data securely across devices.

This may include pet profiles, records, recurring care items, weight records, uploaded files, account profile information, and shared care data.

If you use Guest Mode, your data is primarily stored locally on your device. If you later sign in and choose to sync, your local data may be uploaded to cloud storage.

4. Third-Party Services

KnowPaw uses third-party services to provide app functionality, including:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Firestore
  • Firebase Storage
  • Firebase AI Logic / Google AI
  • Apple Sign In
  • Google Sign-In

These services may process information according to their own privacy policies and terms.

5. Photos, Files, and Health Documents

KnowPaw only accesses photos or files that you choose to upload or attach.

Uploaded pet photos, record photos, profile avatars, and health documents may be stored locally on your device and, if you are signed in, synced to cloud storage.

You should avoid uploading sensitive information unless you are comfortable storing it in the app.

6. Notifications

KnowPaw may use local notifications to remind you about recurring pet care, missed care summaries, or other app-related reminders.

You can manage notification permissions through your device settings.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

We do not use your pet health records for third-party advertising.

We do not share your pet records with other users unless you choose to use shared care features, such as inviting a caregiver to access a pet profile.

8. Data Retention

We keep your information for as long as needed to provide the app's features, maintain your account, and support data sync.

You may delete records, remove pets, or stop using shared care features within the app. Some deleted items may remain temporarily in local storage, backups, or cloud systems before being fully removed.

9. Your Choices

You can:

  • Use the app in Guest Mode
  • Create or sign in to an account
  • Edit or delete pet information
  • Delete individual records
  • Remove uploaded photos or files
  • Manage notification permissions
  • Stop sharing a pet with caregivers
  • Contact us to request help with account or data deletion

10. Children's Privacy

KnowPaw is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

11. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

12. Veterinary Disclaimer

KnowPaw is a pet recordkeeping and care tracking app. It does not provide veterinary diagnosis, treatment, or medical advice.

AI-generated summaries and app insights are for informational purposes only. Always consult a licensed veterinarian for medical concerns about your pet.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we may update the effective date or provide notice in the app.
