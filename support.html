Account and Guest Mode

You can use KnowPaw in Guest Mode without creating an account. Guest Mode keeps your pet records primarily on your device.

If you sign in with Apple, Google, or email, KnowPaw can help sync your data across devices and restore it after signing back in.

Sync and Shared Care

Signed-in accounts can use cloud sync for pets, records, recurring care, and shared care information.

Shared care lets you invite trusted caregivers to help manage a pet profile. Only invite people you trust to view or update that pet's care information.

Records, Reminders, and Widgets

KnowPaw helps track daily care, health notes, weight, medication, supplements, grooming, walks, food, water, pee, poop, and other pet routines.

Recurring care reminders and widgets are designed to make everyday care easier to see and complete. You can manage notification permissions in your device settings.

Photos and Health Files

You can attach pet photos, record photos, profile avatars, and health files that you choose to upload or save in the app.

Please avoid uploading sensitive information unless you are comfortable storing it in KnowPaw.

AI Summaries

Optional AI summaries can help summarize pet records and care patterns. They are intended for organization and reflection, not diagnosis or treatment.

You should always use your own judgment and consult a professional for medical concerns.

Important Disclaimer

KnowPaw is not veterinary advice. For urgent health concerns, contact a licensed veterinarian or emergency veterinary clinic.
